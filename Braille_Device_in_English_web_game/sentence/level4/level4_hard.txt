Every day people have the chance to do something great, but not every person will choose to take that chance and go from where they are to where they can be.
It is not just the people who have knowledge that can change the world, but those who have the will to go out and do something with what they know.
From the moment we are born we can choose to be the kind of people who lift others up, and that choice will have more power than just about anything else we can do.
