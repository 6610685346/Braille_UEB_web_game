Holding on to anger is like grasping a hot coal with the intent of throwing it at someone else; you are the one who gets burned, and the understanding of this truth is the beginning of wisdom.
The spirit of the people is greater than the spirit of the world, and knowledge of this cannot be taken away by those who seek to silence the voices of the many in favor of the few.
Every day is a new beginning, and those who cannot find the strength to start again are not lacking in spirit but in the understanding that greatness is not born in a single moment but in the countless small steps taken with intention and care.
